About the Institute of Digital Risk

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and AI risk roles. We help organisations working in regulated and high-consequence environments build teams that understand both technical systems and real-world risk.

IDR brings together academic insight from leading UK university partners with hands-on industry experience. Our learners work on practical projects, case studies, and simulated incidents so they can move quickly from classroom to live deployment.

By combining education, research, and advisory work, IDR helps close the global skills gap in digital risk, AI governance, and cyber resilience.

Our Model: Train, Innovate, Deploy

IDR’s work is built around three core pillars that support talent, innovation, and impact.

Academy

The IDR Academy delivers intensive programs, bootcamps, and part-time courses for students and working professionals. Participants build foundations in digital risk, cybersecurity, and AI governance through practical case studies.

Innovation & Incubation

Through our Innovation & Incubation stream, IDR explores new models for digital and AI risk, builds prototype tools, and tests emerging governance frameworks. We work with partners to turn research ideas into practical methods.

Advisory Services

Our Advisory Services team helps organisations design and implement risk frameworks aligned with standards such as NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.
